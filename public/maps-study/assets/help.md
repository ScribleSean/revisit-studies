# How to Use This Map Interface

Welcome to the study! This quick guide shows the controls you will need while completing each task.

## 🖱️ Map Exploration:

**🔍 Zoom and Pan**

- **Zoom**: Use your mouse scroll wheel or trackpad to zoom in/out.
- **Pan**: Click and drag the map to move around.

**🎯 County Selection and Removal**

- **To select a county**: Click on a county on the map.
- **To deselect a county**: Click the same county again to remove it.

---

## ✏️ Drawing Tools

For the drawing task, use the drawing controls at the top of the map to highlight regions of interest:

- Drawing: Click on pen to enable drawing. Then click and drag on the map to draw a circle.
- **Undo** ↩️: Undo your last drawing.
- **Redo** ↪️: Redo the last undone drawing.
- **Clear** 🗑️: Remove all drawn shapes from the map

---


## 🎙️ Verbalizing Your Thoughts

This study includes **think-aloud protocols**.

- **Speak aloud** any observations, confusions, or decisions you make.
- You can **pause/resume audio recording** using the **"Recording audio"** button at the top-right of the screen.
- Before proceeding to the next screen, make sure to check the box: **“Have you verbalized your thoughts?”**


---

## ✅ Navigation

- If you have verbalized your thoughts click on the checkbox
- Click **Next** after completing each task to move to the next task.
