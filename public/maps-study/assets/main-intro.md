## In this study, we will ask you to interact with different map types.

Two key parts in this study involve: 

1. Using your microphone to **"think aloud"** to share how you're making sense of the maps. 
2. Completing simple drawing tasks.

Please ensure you have a working **microphone** and are prepared to use your **mouse** to make simple drawings on the map.

You will also complete a _short color vision test_ using color charts.
